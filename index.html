Sofar Ocean Fuel Savings Calculator – How It Works 💡

This calculator helps shipping companies estimate potential fuel cost savings and CO₂ emissions reductions by using Sofar Ocean's Wayfinder platform for optimized voyage planning.

How to Use:

  • 1. Input Your Fleet Details 🚢
    Enter the number of ships, average voyage length, daily fuel consumption, and current fuel price.
  • 2. Adjust the Estimated Savings Percentage 🌱
    The calculator estimates fuel savings between 4% to 10%, based on real-world results from Wayfinder.
  • 3. See Your Savings 📊
    The calculator will automatically show:
    • Total Fuel Cost (Current) 💰
    • Estimated Savings (USD) 💸
    • Fuel Cost with Wayfinder 🔧
    • CO₂ Emissions Reduction (MT) 🌎

Assumptions Made in This Calculator ⚙️

  • Fuel Consumption is Based on Daily Averages ⛽
    The calculator assumes a constant fuel consumption rate (MT/day) for the entire voyage duration.
  • Fuel Price is Static 💰
    The fuel price entered is considered fixed for the voyage. Fluctuations in fuel prices are not accounted for.
  • Estimated Savings Range 🌱
    The fuel savings percentage is estimated between 4% to 10%, based on typical results from Sofar Ocean's Wayfinder platform. Actual savings may vary depending on weather conditions, route complexity, and vessel type.
  • CO₂ Emissions Reduction 🌎
    The calculator assumes that each metric ton of fuel burned produces 3.15 metric tons of CO₂, based on standard shipping industry benchmarks.
